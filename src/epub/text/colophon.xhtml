Honouring the Truth, Reconciling for the Future
was published in 2015 by
The Truth and Reconciliation Commission of Canada.

This ebook was produced for the
TRC Open Source Publishing Project
by
Brendan Brown,
and is based on a PDF version hosted by
The National Centre for Truth and Reconciliation.

The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.

The first edition of this ebook was released on
January 1, 1900, 12:00 a.m.
You can check for updates to this ebook, view its revision history, or download it for different ereading systems at
github.com/bwhittakerb/truth-and-reconciliation-commission-of-canada_honouring-the-truth-reconciling-for-the-future.
